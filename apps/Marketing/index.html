How it works

Simple for everyone.

Register on rheo.co, pick a plan that fits your volume, and complete your business verification. Takes less than 5 minutes.

Enter pickup and delivery details on the app or web dashboard. Set it and forget it — we handle the rest.

Nearby approved drivers see your job and accept it. You're notified the moment someone is on their way.
